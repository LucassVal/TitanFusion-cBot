# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct - Trading Algorithms Community

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of this algorithmic trading community pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of trading experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and collaborative trading community.

## Our Standards

### Behavior That Strengthens Our Community

* **Sharing knowledge and strategies** openly and transparently
* **Being respectful** of different trading approaches and risk tolerances
* **Providing constructive feedback** on code, strategies, and risk management
* **Acknowledging limitations** in trading strategies and being honest about drawdowns
* **Helping newcomers** understand the risks of algorithmic trading
* **Crediting original work** and respecting intellectual property
* **Maintaining realistic expectations** about trading performance

### Behavior That Harms Our Community

* **Making unrealistic profit claims** or promising guaranteed returns
* **Sharing unverified or misleading backtest results**
* **Promoting excessive risk-taking** or irresponsible leverage
* **Plagiarizing trading strategies** or code without attribution
* **Harassing others** about their trading performance or strategies
* **Revealing sensitive financial information** without consent
* **Providing financial advice** without proper qualifications
* **Using the community for pump-and-dump schemes** or market manipulation

## Technical Collaboration Standards

### We Encourage:
* **Code review** and security audits of trading algorithms
* **Risk disclosure** in strategy discussions
* **Backtest validation** with multiple time periods
* **Paper trading results** before live deployment
* **Documenting edge cases** and failure scenarios

### We Prohibit:
* **Intentional code vulnerabilities** or malicious scripts
* **Undisclosed conflicts of interest**
* **Front-running strategies** or market abuse techniques
* **Hiding significant risks** in trading algorithms

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful to our community's integrity.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Discord/Slack channels
- Code review sessions
- Strategy sharing forums
- Live trading performance discussions
- Any space where community members interact

## Reporting and Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders at **lucassvalerio@gmail.com**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Educational Warning
**Community Impact**: First-time minor violations, unintentional risk misrepresentation, or inexperienced trading advice.

**Consequence**: Private written guidance explaining proper risk disclosure and community standards. Educational resources provided.

### 2. Formal Warning
**Community Impact**: Repeated minor violations, unrealistic performance claims, or inadequate risk disclosure.

**Consequence**: Formal warning with temporary restrictions on strategy sharing. Required mentorship on proper risk communication.

### 3. Temporary Suspension
**Community Impact**: Serious violations including irresponsible leverage promotion, misleading backtests, or repeated unverified claims.

**Consequence**: 30-90 day suspension from all community interactions. All shared content undergoes review before reinstatement.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Community Impact**: Pattern of harmful behavior, intentional risk concealment, market manipulation promotion, or malicious code sharing.

**Consequence**: Permanent ban from all community spaces and platforms.

## Trading-Specific Guidelines

### Risk Management Focus
- Always discuss both potential profits AND risks
- Never guarantee returns or minimize potential losses
- Disclose maximum drawdown and worst-case scenarios
- Share both winning and losing strategies for learning

### Performance Transparency
- Show full equity curves, not just profitable periods
- Disclose backtest parameters and potential overfitting
- Differentiate between backtest and live results
- Acknowledge market regime dependencies

### Code Safety
- Never share code with intentional vulnerabilities
- Include proper error handling and position limits
- Document all known edge cases and failure modes
- Use proper risk per trade calculations
